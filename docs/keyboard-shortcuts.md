# Keyboard shortcuts

TapeSim's replay controls are on the keyboard so you can keep your eyes on the tape.

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| <kbd>Space</kbd> | Play / pause replay |
| <kbd>←</kbd> | Seek **back** 5 seconds |
| <kbd>→</kbd> | Seek **forward** 5 seconds |

When you pause, play, or seek, a brief overlay (`||`, `▶`, `<<`, `>>`) flashes in the centre to confirm the action.

Everything else — placing and cancelling orders, changing speed, order size, venue, and tick size — is done by clicking, on the [ladder](#the-dom-ladder) and in the [toolbar](#the-trading-screen).
