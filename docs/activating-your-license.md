# Activating your license

Sim trading is unlocked with a license. Viewing the tape is always free.

## Where your key comes from

When you start TapeSim through the checkout, **Polar** (our payment provider) issues you a **license key** — a long code that looks like `XXXXXXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXX`. You'll find it in your **Polar customer portal**, linked from the receipt email Polar sends after checkout.

> **7-day free trial:** new subscriptions begin with a 7-day free trial — your key works immediately and you aren't charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime before then (from your Polar customer portal) and you won't be billed.

## Activating

1. Launch TapeSim. On the **Activate TapeSim** screen, paste your key into the field.
2. Click **Activate**. You'll see *"Activating…"* while it checks your key, then the app opens the Library with sim trading unlocked.

If anything's wrong with the key, the reason is shown in red right below the button (for example, an unrecognized key, or one whose subscription is no longer active).

## Using it on more than one machine

A license can be activated on **up to 3 machines**. Activate normally on each one.

If you've **upgraded or replaced a PC** and run out of activations, you can free a slot yourself: open your **Polar customer portal**, find your license key, and **deactivate** the machine you no longer use. Your next activation then has room. (If you'd rather we help, email **support@tapesim.app**.)

## Staying activated

After you activate once, TapeSim remembers it — you won't have to paste the key again on later launches. It quietly re-checks your license in the background when needed.

**Offline?** That's fine. Once activated, TapeSim keeps working without an internet connection. If your session ever goes stale while you're offline, there's a short grace window; reconnect once and it refreshes automatically.

## Cancelling

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Polar customer portal. You keep access until the end of the period you've paid for. See the [Refund Policy](refund.html) for details.
