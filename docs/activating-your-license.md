# Activating your license

Sim trading is unlocked with a license. Viewing the tape is always free.

## Where your key comes from

When you buy TapeSim through the checkout, **Lemon Squeezy** (our payment provider) emails you a **license key** — a long code that looks like `XXXXXXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXX`.

> **Beta note:** TapeSim is free during the beta — no purchase needed. Activate sim trading with the shared beta key `7645AE99-A293-4DAF-8C3A-C91F97BA8469`. (Paid, per-customer licenses arrive when TapeSim leaves beta.)

## Activating

1. Launch TapeSim. On the **Activate TapeSim** screen, paste your key into the field.
2. Click **Activate**. You'll see *"Activating…"* while it checks your key, then the app opens the Library with sim trading unlocked.

If anything's wrong with the key, the reason is shown in red right below the button (for example, an unrecognized or wrong-store key).

## Using it on more than one machine

A license can be activated on **up to 3 machines**. Activate normally on each one.

If you've **upgraded or replaced a PC** and run out of activations, or you see:

> *"This license is activated on a different machine. Re-activate here to move it (uses one of your 3 activations), or continue as viewer."*

…you can either **Activate** on the new machine (which uses one of your activations), or email **support@tapesim.app** and we'll extend your limit or free up the old machine.

## Staying activated

After you activate once, TapeSim remembers it — you won't have to paste the key again on later launches. It quietly re-checks your license in the background when needed.

**Offline?** That's fine. Once activated, TapeSim keeps working without an internet connection. If your session ever goes stale while you're offline, there's a short grace window; reconnect once and it refreshes automatically.

## Cancelling

You can cancel your subscription anytime from the link in your Lemon Squeezy receipt. You keep access until the end of the period you've paid for. See the [Refund Policy](refund.html) for details.
